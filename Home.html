Not Ferrari's Year

Scuderia Ferrari and Charles Leclerc have  lost the hope to win this Championship

The Maranello-based team started the season strongly with two wins in the first three races but things have not gone their way since. In the last 12 rounds of racing, Ferrari has only won two races, in Silverstone and Spielberg.The Italian outfit has been plagued by reliability issues while also shooting themselves in the foot with their strategy calls on multiple occasions this season. Most recently, they compromised Carlos Sainz's chances in the Dutch GP, calling him in for a pit-stop without having the rear-left tire ready, resulting in a 12.7 second stationary stop for him.
